About RPNow

RPNow is the Dead-Simple Roleplay Chatroom Service. It is the only roleplay site that requires no user registration, supports multiple characters per user, keeps your roleplays permanents, and allows you to download the complete transcripts as text files.

To start a roleplay, simply type in a title, and press the begin button! It's that simple! Don't believe us? Try it!

RPNow was written almost exclusively by Nigel Nelson, with special help from Hannah Liddell.
